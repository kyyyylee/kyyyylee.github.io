Massachusetts has all the geographical features of the other states, from skiing and fall foliage drives in the western Berkshire Mountains to the beaches of Cape Cod and vacation islands of Martha's Vineyard and Nantucket.

The birth of America comes to life as you travel through Boston, Massachusetts. Discover the American trail of freedom while exploring one of the nation's oldest and most historic cities.

Boston is not only one of America's oldest cities, it's also one of the most walkable, and we'd even go as far as saying it's one of the easiest to explore.

In Boston, Massachusetts, you can eat hearty New England cuisine, get hands-on at world-class museums, and soak in skyline views all in a city with deep history and a lively atmosphere.
